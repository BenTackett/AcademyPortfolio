About My School

I go to Academy Highschool, which is a STEAM based (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Math) school with a project based curriculum. In these projects, we get assigned a group of 3-5 classmates who we spend the entire project with. These projects usually last 1-2 months, and we have 5 or 6 a year. This site contains reviews and reflections of all projects to date.
